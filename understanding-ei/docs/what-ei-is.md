# What EI Is

## Overview

Employment Insurance (EI) is a public program in Canada with rules, definitions, and processes published through official sources.

## Plain-language summary

This project explains EI concepts in general terms and points readers to official material for exact wording and legal authority.

## What to verify in official sources

- Current legal text
- Policy and administrative guidance
- Tribunal decisions and procedural references
