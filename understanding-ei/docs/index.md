# Understanding EI

!!! warning "Unofficial informational resource"
    This website is independent and **not** an official Government of Canada, ESDC, Service Canada, or Social Security Tribunal website.

    It is for **general educational information only** and **not legal advice**. For official requirements, rely on official government and tribunal sources. For advice about your own situation, consult a qualified professional.

## Purpose

Understanding EI explains public Employment Insurance (EI) concepts in plain language. The goal is to help readers understand common EI topics, terms, and decision structures using publicly available information.

## What this site is for

- Explaining EI concepts at a high level
- Showing how topic areas are commonly organized
- Pointing to official sources for rules and decisions
- Building a clear glossary of terms used in EI materials

## What this site is not for

- Not legal advice
- Not case-specific guidance
- Not advocacy strategy
- Not insider or unofficial interpretations
