# What EI Cares About and Why

## Overview

This section introduces major EI decision areas at a high level. It focuses on understanding broad categories and why they appear in EI processes.

## Common decision categories

- Job separation circumstances
- Voluntary leaving
- Misconduct
- Availability for work
- Training or schooling status
- Earnings while on claim

## Why these categories matter (high-level)

Each category can affect eligibility, entitlement, or ongoing claim status. This site explains structure and terminology only.
